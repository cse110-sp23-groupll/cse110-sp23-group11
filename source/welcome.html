What is a Tarot card?

A Tarot card is a playing card used to divine future. There are a total of 78 cards in the standard tarot deck, but we use the 22 Major Arcana cards here.

What is a Tarot card reading?

Tarot card readings are the practice of using the Tarot card deck to provide insight and guidance for one's future.

How do I begin?

First, think of a question or topic you would like guidance for. Then click the "Start Reading" button. You'll select three cards and be taken to your fortune. Don't forget to click on the "Recipe" button to get a fun recipe with your fortune!

What if I don't like my fortune?

Simply click on the "New Reading" button at the top right of the fortune screen and select three new cards.
